Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Overview

Freakie Dogs Analytics ("the App") is a private internal tool operated by Freakie Dogs restaurant chain (El Salvador). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data collected through TikTok API integrations.

2. Data We Collect

When the @freakiedogs TikTok account authorizes the App, we collect the following data exclusively from Freakie Dogs' own TikTok account:

  • Account profile information (username, display name, follower count)
  • Video performance metrics (views, likes, comments, shares, creation date)
  • Video metadata (title, description, cover image URL)

We do not collect any data from third-party TikTok users, followers, or any other TikTok accounts.

3. How We Use Data

All collected data is used exclusively for:

  • Internal business performance reporting
  • Social media analytics for Freakie Dogs marketing team
  • Strategic decision-making by company management

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored in Freakie Dogs' private Supabase database with row-level security policies. Access is restricted to authorized employees only. Data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

5. TikTok Data

Our use of TikTok API data complies with TikTok's Platform Terms and Developer Policies. We only access data that has been explicitly authorized by the Freakie Dogs account holder. TikTok users can revoke app permissions at any time through TikTok's app settings.

6. Data Retention

Social media metrics are retained indefinitely for historical trend analysis. Account holders may request deletion of their data by contacting us.

7. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any data obtained through TikTok API to third parties, advertisers, or partners.

8. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact: freakiedogs@gmail.com
